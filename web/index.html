How to Get Your OLX Location URL

1

Open OLX & Click "For Rent: Houses & Apartments"

Go to olx.in and click the category from the top navigation bar.

2

Select Your Exact Neighborhood from the Sidebar

On the left side, expand the LOCATIONS tree and click your exact neighborhood (e.g., Hiranmagri, Sundarpur).

3

Copy the URL from Your Browser's Address Bar

The URL will look like olx.in/en-in/sundarpur_g5343637/for-rent-houses-apartments_c1723. Copy and paste it into the box.
